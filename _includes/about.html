About

When I was 7 years old my dream job was to grow up and become a super hero with magical powers, much like a wizard (e.g. Pug from "The Magician"). As I grew older, the pressures of reality kicked in and the dream to become a wizard began to fade away.

Then I heard a quote from Arthur C Clark, "any sufficiently advanced technology should be indistinguishable from magic". After hearing this, I was reinspired to continue chasing my dreams and thus the technology consultant (wizard) journey begun.
